CHICKEN POPCORN

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless,boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black peeper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • oil for frying

Preparation

  1. Chop boneless chicken breasts into bite-size pieces.
  2. Mix flour, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl. Beat eggs, butter milk, lemon juice together in a separate bowl.
  3. Heat 1 1/2 to 2 inches of oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  4. Dip the chicken pieces into the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, and again into the flour mixture.
  5. Test the oil to make sure it is hot enough: a small amount of flour dropped in should sizzle.
  6. Place 10 pieces of chicken into the hot oil;cook until golden brown, about 8 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oil and drain on a paper towel lined plate and season with salt.
  8. Repeat with remaining chicken.
